What are the rules?

1. To be eligible for the competition, you need to be a Boosteroid active subscriber.

2. You can participate in just one or in all challenges - the more the better, as your chances to win will grow.

3. Record your gameplay on Boosteroid while performing the tasks from the challenge (make sure that your video captures the Boosteroid app when you start playing).

4. Publish your video on YouTube or TikTok open to the public. Don't forget to put all the hashtags listed in the tasks on the challenge cards.

5. Fill in the form below to apply for the competition, make sure to fill in your Boosteroid account email, nickname for the lottery, the challenge that you have chosen and add the link to your video.

6. Join our Discord to watch the live giveaway event and see who the lucky winners are.

7. If you win, expect an email from our team to the email address you left in the application form.

8. The winner will be able to choose 3 games, one from each list as they appear on this webpage.

9. You cannot choose games that are not from the list, two games from the same list or get a money equivalent of the games you have chosen. You will receive your game codes via email after you choose your prizes.

10. The deadline to submit your applications for the challenges is Sunday, June 25, 23:59 UTC.
The winners will be announced during a live draw on Friday, June 30, 12:00 UTC (all details can be found on Discord).
